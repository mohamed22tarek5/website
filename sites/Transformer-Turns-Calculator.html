Calculation Formula

N = V × 10⁸ / (4.44 × f × B × A)

Where:

  • N = Number of turns
  • V = Voltage (RMS)
  • f = Frequency (Hz)
  • B = Flux density (Tesla)
  • A = Core area (cm²)
