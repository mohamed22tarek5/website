Transformer Design Tips

  • Use Bmax values between 1.0-1.5T for silicon steel cores to avoid saturation
  • Add a 20% safety margin to wire gauge for thermal headroom
  • Higher frequency transformers can use fewer turns and smaller cores
  • Always round turns up to the nearest whole number
  • Consider copper losses (I²R) when selecting wire gauge for high-current windings
