Ohm's Law Triangle (V, I, R)

From this triangle:

  • V = I × R
  • I = V ÷ R
  • R = V ÷ I

Power Triangle (P, V, I)

From this triangle:

  • P = V × I
  • V = P ÷ I
  • I = P ÷ V

Formulas Used

Quantity Formula Notes
Voltage (V) V = I × R Ohm's law basic form
Current (I) I = V ÷ R Rearranged from V = I × R
Resistance (R) R = V ÷ I Rearranged from V = I × R
Power (P) P = V × I Real power in DC circuits
Power (P) P = I² × R Useful when V is unknown
Power (P) P = V² ÷ R Useful when I is unknown
