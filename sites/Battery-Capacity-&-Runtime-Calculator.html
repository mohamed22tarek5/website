How Battery Runtime is Calculated

  • Battery Energy (Wh) = Battery Voltage (V) × Battery Capacity (Ah)
  • Runtime (hours) = (Battery Energy × Efficiency) / Load Power
  • For lead-acid batteries, it's recommended to not discharge below 50% to extend battery life
  • Lithium batteries can typically be discharged to 80-90% of their capacity
  • Actual runtime may vary based on temperature, battery age, and other factors

Tips for Solar Projects

  • Add 20-30% extra capacity to account for cloudy days
  • Consider using a battery monitor for accurate state-of-charge readings
  • For critical loads, consider having a backup generator or grid connection
  • Regularly maintain your batteries to ensure maximum lifespan
  • Size your solar panels to recharge your batteries within a sunny day
